Clair's Story

I healed from my issues with anxiety! For years, my mind raced faster than I could keep up with, turning small moments into horribly overwhelming storms. But I learned to pause, breathe, and chellenge those thoughts. Therapy and self-compassion changed everything. Now, I still have occasional bad days and bouts of anxiety, but they don't control me anymore. Life feels lighter, and I can finally enjoy life in the present!

Dave's Story

I healed from my issues with my ADHD! As a business owner, I used to feel scatterbrained and overwhelmed all thw time, constantly starting things I couldn't finish. It hurt my confidence, and really hurt my clients! But I've learnt how my brain works, build systems that support me, and stopped fighting myself. Now I manage my ADHD with structure and patience. My business is thriving and so am I! I finally feel in control of my own success.

Teresa's Story

I healed from my postpartum depression! After my son was born, I felt so lost and disconnnected, like I wasn't the mother he deserved. Getting help was the hardest step, but it changed everything! With time, support, and self-love, I found myself again. Today, my son just turned 8, and celebrating with him filled me with pure joy. I'm present, grateful, and so so proud of how far we've both come!
