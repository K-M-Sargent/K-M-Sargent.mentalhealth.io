What we aim to Accomplish:

Moon Clinic exists to make mental health knowledge more accessible, approachable, and misinformation-free for everyone everywhere. Our goal is to make it possible for people to learn, reflect, and help themselves and everyone around them.

Through this website, we hope to encourage open conversations, reduce isolation, and motivate people to take steps towards healing and happiness. Whether someone is struggling or helping someone around them, Moon Clinic provides tools and reassurance that they are not alone.

Ultimately, we strive to spread understanding and knowledge worldwide, helping build a more informed and empathetic community where mental health is treated with care and respect.
